About Me

Born in the sunny city of Mombasa, Kenya, I have always been crazy about football - especially when it comes to Manchester United. I have gone through every high and low imaginable, from thrilling victories to heartbreaking defeats. But that's the life of a true fan - riding the rollercoaster of emotions, knowing that every peak and valley makes the journey all the more unforgettable.

My love for football naturally fed into my passion for data analysis. Just as I track every stat and pattern in a match, I approach data with the same meticulous attention to detail. Whether it's analyzing game strategies or crunching numbers in a professional setting, I find excitement in uncovering patterns and making sense of the data. For me, football and data are two sides of the same coin - both are about understanding the bigger picture through the small details.
